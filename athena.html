01 — GAMEPLAY

AI that actually behaves like it should.

The base game treats wildlife as scenery. Athena gives it a brain. Creatures pathfind around real terrain, react to what's happening around them, and move in groups with leaders and followers. You decide what each species does, how many can spawn, and where they belong.

Real pathfinding with situational decision-making and per-species personality.
Group behavior — herds, packs, leaders, followers — not just wandering NPCs.
Population caps, locks, and ecosystem balancing per species.
Spawn protection that's PvP-only, configurable, and breaks the moment you attack.
02 — VOICE

Hear them the way they're standing.

Most "proximity voice" mods give you a volume slider. Athena gives you a soundstage. You hear other players from the actual direction they're in, with distance falloff and terrain muffling that sells the world. And it's locked down — voice can't be weaponized to probe positions or sniff for hidden players.

True 3D positional audio — direction, distance, and terrain matter.
Cross-species local chat for mixed herds and reduced global-chat toxicity.
Clean audio quality with secure, abuse-hardened sign-in.
Designed so voice can't be exploited as a positional radar.
03 — ADMINISTRATION

Run your server from anywhere.

Logging into the game just to ban someone is a tax on your time. Athena exposes everything — spawning, kicks, bans, config changes, garage edits, AI control — through web dashboards and Discord integrations. Your staff gets the tools the official server never gave them, plus tiered roles so nobody has more power than they should.

Tiered staff roles with fine-grained permissions. Trust calibrated.
Bans and mutes with reasons, durations, and persistence across restarts.
Drive your server from web dashboards or your Discord — no in-game login.
Connection optimization and automatic cleanup of dead/zombie sockets.
04 — SCRIPTING

Build your own features. No rebuilds. No downtime.

Athena ships with a built-in, safe scripting layer. Want a custom welcome command? A reaction to a kill streak? Automated nightly events? Write a script, save it, and it's live. No compile step. No restart. No background in C++ required. Your server starts behaving like a product you're shipping instead of a config file you're praying over.

Custom chat commands, event reactions, and timed automation.
Saves apply instantly. No rebuild. No restart. No downtime.
Safe execution layer — scripts can't crash the server.
Designed so non-programmers can ship working features.
