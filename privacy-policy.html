This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our website and game (the “Service”). Our game integrates Epic Games Online Services (“Epic Online Services” or “EOS”) and is built using Unreal Engine.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and manage our services, we collect the following information:

  • Username
  • IP address
  • Technical data about your operating system and runtime environment

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for internal purposes such as user account management, providing access to our services, preventing abuse, and communicating with you regarding your account.

3. Data Sharing and Sale

We will never sell, rent, lease, or give away your personal data to any third party. Your information is used solely for the internal operation of this website and game. We may share only what is necessary with service providers strictly to operate features of the Service (see Section 8 for Epic Online Services and Unreal Engine details).

4. Data Retention

We will retain your information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, or to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. Your Right to Data Deletion

You have the right to request the complete deletion of your personal data from our systems. To do so, please send an email request to [email protected] from the email address associated with your account.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 (“Children”). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Child has provided us with Personal Information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We implement a variety of industry-standard security measures and best practices to maintain the safety and integrity of your data.

8. Epic Online Services (EOS) & Unreal Engine

Our game is developed with Unreal Engine and integrates Epic Online Services (EOS) to provide features such as multiplayer, matchmaking, achievements, leaderboards, voice chat, anti-cheat, and account linking (depending on the features we enable).

To operate these features, limited data may be exchanged with Epic’s services (for example, technical identifiers, online IDs, session tokens, and networking information). This exchange is strictly for enabling EOS functionality within our game and is not sold.

  • We only transmit the minimum information required to enable the EOS features we use.
  • Data processed by Epic is handled under Epic’s own terms and privacy notices.
  • Unreal Engine tooling may collect crash or performance diagnostics to improve engine stability.

For questions about how EOS or Unreal Engine process data, please consult Epic’s official documentation and privacy notices. You can also contact us at [email protected] and we’ll help route your request where appropriate.
